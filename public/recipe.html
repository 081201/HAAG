Homemade Chicken Noodle Soup

This classic homemade chicken noodle soup is made from scratch in 30 minutes! It's loaded with tender pieces of chicken, hearty vegetables and fluffy egg noodles. This easy soup recipe is perfect for a quick weeknight meal or a cozy weekend dinner.
